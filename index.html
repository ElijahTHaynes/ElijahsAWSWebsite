Zombicide is a zombie havoc boardgame by Guillotine Games

Zombicide is a boardgame with miniatures for 1 to 6 players, age 14 and up. It's a great experience solo or with friends!

Click here to visit the official Zombicide Website!

Zombicide Expansions

The Zombicide Game carries a bunch of expansions:

Washington ZC

Washington Z.C. Expansion introduces for the very first time for Zombicide a story-driven campaign, full of flavorful and impactful surprises and choices.

Fort Hendrix

Fort Hendrix is a Zombicide experience like no other, featuring a full campaign with 6 new survivors set in a U.S. military base.

Rio Z Janeiro

Zombicide: Rio Z Janeiro is a new campaign expansion for Zombicide 2nd edition. The setting is the busy streets of Rio de Janeiro where the Survivors must make it out of town as quickly as they can.
